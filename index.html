I'm a UX and Market Researcher with a background spanning e-grocery (Sayurbox), job-market tech (Glints), and smartphone businesses (vivo). I specialize in delivering consumer insight with recommendations that are built to be acted on — not filed away. My work spans foundational, evaluative, and innovation-focused studies — from early-stage exploration to post-launch measurement.

Earlier in my career, my work covered product testing, segmentation, positioning, brand health tracking, conjoint analysis, and campaign evaluation for vivo. I then led UX research at Sayurbox across B2C, B2B, and Product Ops, and supported the commercial team with price perception studies, category evaluations, and CSAT tracking that informed stakeholder decisions.

Since November 2024, I've rejoined vivo on the iQOO side, leading target audience strategy, post-launch evaluation, and persona research across the full iQOO lineup.
